Overview

This app is designed to help you plan ahead, not just track spending.

  • Day-before-payday focus — ensures your balance stays safe before income arrives
  • Forward projection — see up to 24 months ahead
  • What If testing — safely explore savings or spending changes
  • Local data only — nothing is stored online
↑ Back to top

Quick Start (2 minutes)

  1. Set your start date and opening balance
  2. Add your regular income (salary/pension)
  3. Add your regular expenses
  4. Check Lowest before next income
  5. Use What If to increase savings safely

Set up your budget

Enter a start date and opening balance, then save.

If your bank shows £150 and you have £50 of transactions on that day, your opening balance should be £200.

Add categories, then transactions. The projection updates immediately.

Summary Report

Shows total income and expenses over 24 months.

  • Grouped by category
  • Frequency-adjusted (monthly vs 4-weekly)
  • Includes totals and averages
Use this to understand how much is available for savings.
↑ Back to top

What If

Tests how much you can safely save each month.

  1. Click What If
  2. Select a start date
  3. Click OK

The app calculates a safe monthly saving amount using a buffer.

If balances go negative, your plan is not safe.
